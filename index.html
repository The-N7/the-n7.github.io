About Empower Ethiopia

Empower Ethiopia connects the rural with the urban through fashion design.

It is a shared non-profit company that aims in the development of Ethiopia in the fashion industry, though Ethiopia has indeed had a role in the industry in past times, her involvement in present times has been decreasing.

But empower Ethiopia has got a mission to make mother land Ethiopia at the top yet again!!
